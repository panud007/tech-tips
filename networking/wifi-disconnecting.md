## âœ… Solusi: Matikan Power Saving

Windows suka iseng mematikan WiFi adapter buat hemat baterai.

1. Buka **Device Manager** (`Win + X` > Device Manager).
2. Expand **Network adapters**.
3. Klik kanan adapter WiFi kamu > **Properties**.
4. Pindah ke tab **Power Management**.
5. **UNCHECK** (Hapus centang) pada:
   `Allow the computer to turn off this device to save power`
6. OK & Restart.
